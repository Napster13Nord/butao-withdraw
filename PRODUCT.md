# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
WooCommerce store owners in Portugal needing quick legal compliance with the European Directive 2023/2673 to avoid heavy fines (up to 4% turnover or 2 million euros) and automated web sweep detection.

## Product Purpose
Provide a high-conversion, fast-loading sales landing page that sells a WooCommerce installation service for the mandatory "Livre Resolução / Arrependimento" button for €100 with a 24-hour delivery guarantee.

## Brand Personality
- Authority & Security (Trustworthy, professional, digital protection)
- Urgency (24-hour delivery, active threat of audits/fines)
- Transparency (Fixed cost, clear benefit checklist)

## Anti-references
- Soft/low-contrast text "for elegance" that makes it hard to read.
- Generic SaaS cream-colored templates (SaaS clichés).
- Overly rounded borders (keep radii professional, max 12-16px).
- Sketchy SVG illustrations or cheap clip-art.

## Design Principles
- **Commanding Authority**: Establish compliance necessity instantly with professional dark cyber-security visual cues.
- **Unambiguous Urgency**: Highlight the 24h delivery and immediate legal risks without making the interface feel cheap.
- **Frictionless Conversion**: Lead directly to the Formspree capture with clear input fields, no distracting elements, and auto-validation states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio >= 4.5:1 for body and placeholder elements.
- Accessible keyboard navigation for the Formspree interactive form.
- Safe animations with `prefers-reduced-motion` compliance.
